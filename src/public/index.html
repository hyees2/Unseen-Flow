ABOUT

Overview:

This platform does not view the border space around us as a simple geographical border or physical barrier. In today's world, borders are made up of complex and constantly changing fluid systems that span a variety of fields, including information, politics, and economics. To explore this, the platform classifies borders into ten types, illustrating how each is constructed and operates within multi-layered contexts of visibility and physicality. This allows for a deeper understanding of the nature of borders and the complexity of the systems in which they operate.

General Information Sources:

The information compiled on this site draws from a range of sources that document not only the geographical placement of borders, but also their historical origins, structural forms, methods of crossing, and the political, social, and administrative mechanisms behind them.
