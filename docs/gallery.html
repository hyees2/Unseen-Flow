ABOUT

This platform does not view the border space around us as a simple geographical border or physical barrier. In today's world, borders are made up of complex and constantly changing fluid systems that span a variety of fields, including information, politics, and economics. To explore this, the platform classifies borders into ten types, illustrating how each is constructed and operates within multi-layered contexts of visibility and physicality. This allows us to gain a deeper understanding of the nature of borders and the complexity of the systems we are part of.
